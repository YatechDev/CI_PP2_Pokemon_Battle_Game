Welcome Adventurer!

"Pokemon Battle Game" is a simple and enjoyable game based on the popular game "Rock, Paper, Scissors!" But in this case, instead of the standard options, we choose our favorite Pokemons!

The game was created for educational purposes and is a programming fundamentals project, in which you can have fun in the company of iconic creatures with a touch of nostalgia in the background.

Will you be able to achieve victory and become a master of the "Pokemon Battle Game"? Test your skills and find out for yourself!


Choose your game!


If you want to play without significant challenges, we recommend choosing the easy mode! However, if you're looking for a more demanding adventure, we encourage you to select the hard mode! Keep in mind that the choice of game mode can affect the level of enjoyment and excitement you experience during gameplay, so it's worth considering your options carefully.



Click on the left or right image to choose the level!





RULES



FIRE ⋙ GRASS

GRASS ⋙ WATER

WATER ⋙ FIRE



In the Pokemon Battle Game, the player fights against the computer.

In case of a battle between two identical pokemon, the result is a draw.

The game is won by the person who first wins 20 battles. Good luck in the game!

Enjoy!

About Me



YatechDev

I am a fresh programming student who continuously improving my skills & passion. I am glad that I can share my projects with others and see the impressions my ideas evoke in users.

Pokemon Battle Game is a project that was created as part of the second module "Java Script Essential" on the "Code Institute" platform. The idea for the game arose from my love of Pokemon and classic games, and my goal was to create a simple and enjoyable experience that would allow users to relive carefree childhood moments.
